recipe

  • 2 medium potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 6 eggs
  • Salt
  1. Cut the potatoes into thin, small slices.
  2. Pour plenty of olive oil in a pan so that the potatoes are submerged and put it on high heat.
  3. Then put the pan over medium-high heat to cook the potatoes.
  4. When they are soft, they are drained with the kitchen shovel and moved away.
  5. Cut the onion into squares.
  6. Pour a splash of oil into the pan. When it is hot add the onion, put it over medium-low heat and add salt to the onion. Cook it until soft.
  7. Beat the eggs and add salt.
  8. Mix everything in a pan over high heat. As soon as it is hot we put it on medium-high heat and stir everything until it begins to cook.
  9. Turn the tortilla until it is golden brown on both sides.
